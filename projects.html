TSSI – Tractive System Status Indicator

I redesigned our Formula SAE team’s TSSI to be fully waterproof, structurally stronger, and significantly more compact. The new housing uses resin printing for tight tolerances, integrates optimized O-ring sealing, and mounts directly to welded roll-hoop tabs per updated FSAE rules. By reducing surface area, improving aerodynamics, and accommodating evolving 12–24 V electrical standards, the final design delivers a durable, serviceable, and race-ready indicator system.

Using iterative SolidWorks layout, internal load-bearing supports, and a comprehensive waterproofing strategy, I produced a compact, structurally reinforced, carbon-fiber–ready enclosure that integrates cleanly with the vehicle and supports reliable electronics operation during competition.
